How does it work?

Suppoyo. is a contract creator that allows two people to invest a percentage of their incomes in one another for mutual benefit. Each person will also invest support (networking, referrals, project help, etc.) in the other person in order to help one another. We are currently in the testing phase and are recruiting participants to join our study.
